The GOAT Case

Across 13 Chicago Bulls seasons, Michael Jordan delivered a level of dominance unmatched in NBA history. Six championships, six Finals MVPs, five MVPs, ten scoring titles, elite efficiency, and all-time defensive impact — all from a guard who led the league in usage and responsibility.

His postseason consistency, two separate three-peats, and unmatched two-way excellence form a résumé that stands alone. No player has ever combined scoring, efficiency, defense, leadership, and championship success at this level.

This isn’t nostalgia — it’s the numbers.
